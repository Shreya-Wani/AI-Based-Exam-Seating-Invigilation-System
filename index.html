NP-Completeness Discussion

Why This Problem is NP-Complete

The perfect exam seating problem ‚Äî where no two students sharing a subject sit in the same hall ‚Äî is equivalent to the Graph Coloring Problem.

  • Each student is a "node"
  • An "edge" exists between students who share at least one subject
  • Assigning halls = coloring nodes so no adjacent nodes have same color

Since Graph Coloring is NP-Complete, we use heuristic algorithms (like our conflict-avoidance greedy approach) to find practical, near-optimal solutions in polynomial time.

This demonstrates how ADA helps solve real-world NP-Complete problems!
